#### Plagiarism

Don't copy from this template(except for the formatting), the examples provided, or from other websites (unless it's contact information). Plagiarism will be rejected immediately.

#### Structure

You don't have to use the exact H2s provided in this template, and you don't have to follow this template religiously if it doesn't fit the context. You can change the form of the H2s, but don't forget to include core keywords or keyword variations in them whenever you see a good opportunity.

Most of the provided H2s are just examples - It would be much better if you could think of different ones and implement keywords in them.

Make sure that you shuffle the H2s - they shouldn't be in the same order in each article. However, do keep the flow of the text in mind and make sure the H2s follow one another naturally for added value.

#### Keywords

The focus should always be on the provided keywords. Don't follow the template blindly - implement as many keyword variations as you can instead.

### Metadata

Add the meta title and meta description at the top of your article. They have to contain the core keyword (your topic aka the name of your doc). You can follow the template or come up with your own, but don't count them in your article's word count.

#### Meta Title

keyword + something creative and attention-grabbing.

Example: How to Fax Without a Fax Machine [Best Tips]

> Meta title - strive for 30-60 characters, use title case.

#### Meta Description

Keyword + something creative + featured in (pick two or three):

List

List

List

Example: Looking for the best fax service? Send faxes online and without a fax machine anytime and anywhere! Featured in Vice and Forbes!

Example 2: Send faxes online and without a fax machine for next to nothing! Featured in Newsweek, CNN, CNBC, etc!

> Meta description - no shorter than 130 or longer than 160 characters, use sentence case. Look at meta descriptions of our competitors' articles you find for the main keyword to see what ranks well. You can use thruuu to do so. CLICKBAIT is your goal.

### H1: [Keyword] + Something Creative

Example H1: How to Send a Fax Without a Fax Machine Fast and Easy

Give the reader a short and engaging introduction to the topic. You can identify the problem the reader might have and tell them what we've got just the right solution to it.

IMPORTANT: Make sure you use the core keyword somewhere in the introduction (within the first 30-50 words) and bold the sentence that contains it. You should also add as many of the content-series-mandatory internal links as you can at the beginning of the article. This doesn't mean you should force it - if some of the required links don't flow well with the rest of the intro, skip them and save them for later.

> Some of the H2 examples won't work for every article. pay attention to your core keyword and keyword variations more than the template - they will help you a lot.

### H2: The Problem With Fax Machines

List all of the problems that come with fax machine usage, i.e. bad image quality, troubleshooting, etc. Present online fax as a much better alternative.

You can change this H2 in accordance with your topic - it doesn't have to be the same as this one. For example, if you're writing the how-to-fax-from-home article, you can use its send fax from cell phone keyword variation as the H2.

### H2: How Does Online Fax Work?

Give the reader a brief explanation of how online fax works. You can use this H2 to list a few advantages of online fax, but don't make it too long. The reader isn't here to find out what the benefits and drawbacks are - we just need to convince them that our product is the way to go.

You can say that fax is:

More secure for sending sighed documents than email

Integrated with the internet and convenient

Faster than mailing a physical letter

Essential for medical, legal, or government documents.

Online faxing can be done in a couple of different ways - through a service provider or via email. As far as the letter is concerned, the way it works is by you attaching whatever document you want to send to the email. This method is unsafe because your email account could get hacked, for example

IMPORTANT: Don't just rewrite this section - think about the reader's pain points and your keyword variations. Do you feel like they need to know how to send a fax at this point in the article? If so, take the how-to-send-fax article and use one of its keyword variations as an H2. It could be "how to send fax from home,' for example.

### H2: How To Send a Fax on Your Own

Here, you can list some of the other available options. Mention that the reader can send a fax using Microsoft Windows' Fax and Scan, a fax machine, some other apps, etc. After listing these methods, you should tell the reader about their disadvantages.

For example, Fax and Scan require you to have your PC connected to a phone line and other fax apps (our competition) are way too expensive. Fax machines are archaic, and it's clear that they will do another but waste the reader's time.

This H2 is the perfect place to add internal links to other articles. If you're writing the How-to-get-a-fax-number article, you can add links to fax-app, fax-from-iPhone, how-to-fax-from-phone, etc.

### H2: Other Ways To Send a Fax

You can mention email here, but make sure to let the reader know why it's bad. Explain how email providers may store the reader's data and how someone might hack their email.

Use this H2 to reach the word count.

This is also a great opportunity for internal linking - use links such as google fax, fax from iPhone, email to fax Gmail, windows 10 fax, send faxes from mac and google voice fax.
